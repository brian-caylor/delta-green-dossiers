# Delta Green Dossiers — Quick Start

*Last updated: 2026-04-20 · [What's new](CHANGELOG.md)*

Hey — thanks for jumping in. This is a web-based character sheet for our
Delta Green games. Works on phones, tablets, laptops. Your sheets sync to
the cloud so you can hop between devices.

## Get in

1. Open **https://dgfieldguide.oddlyuseful.app** in any modern browser
   (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — the newer the better).
2. Hit **SIGN IN WITH GOOGLE** and pick whichever Google account you want
   tied to your agents. Anything goes — it just has to be the same one
   you sign in with every time.
3. You'll land on the Roster. It's empty on day one.

## Install it like a real app (optional but nice)

It's a PWA, so you can "Add to Home Screen" and launch it like a native
app. Best offline support too.

- **iPhone / iPad (Safari)**: Share button → *Add to Home Screen*.
- **Android / Chrome**: tap the ⋮ menu → *Install app* (or *Add to Home
  Screen*).
- **Desktop Chrome / Edge**: address bar has a little "install" icon at
  the right end → click it.

## Make your first agent

From the Roster:

- **+ NEW AGENT** → walks you through a short wizard (name, profession,
  stats, skills, bonds) and drops you straight onto a filled-out sheet.
- **IMPORT AGENT** → if you already have one saved as JSON or an
  official Delta Green fillable PDF, dump it in here.

Want to just freeform it? Open the sidebar (◁ arrow on the left) while on
the sheet and hit **+ NEW AGENT** there — skips the wizard, blank sheet.

## The sheet at a glance

Five tabs across the top:

- **PERSONAL** — name, profession, bonds, motivations, disorders,
  adaptation tracking, unnatural encounters.
- **STATS** — six core stats (auto-derive HP/WP/SAN/BP max). SAN events
  fire when current SAN drops. *Project onto a Bond* lives here.
- **SKILLS** — everything the core book ships with, plus room for custom
  skills. Click the dice icon to roll d100 vs. the skill. Critical/
  fumble detection is automatic.
- **COMBAT** — weapons, ammo, armor, wounds. Has a quick-reference bar
  at the top so you don't have to tab-hop mid-fight.
- **NOTES** — personal notes, home-life, special training, session log.
  Every stat change and roll outcome auto-logs here with a timestamp.

## The things I want you to actually try

- **Lower your current SAN a point or two**. The SAN event flow fires —
  pick a threat category, walk through temp insanity / repress / breaking
  point as applicable. It's faster than flipping the rulebook.
- **Project onto a bond** (Stats tab) when shit goes sideways.
- **Check the "Failed" box** next to any skill you fail during a
  session. When the mission's done, hit **END MISSION** at the top
  right — it rolls 1d4 advancement on every failed skill at once.
- **Theme switcher**: top bar has **MANILA / BONE / FIELD**. FIELD is
  the retro terminal green thing. Bone is for printing. Pick whichever.
  Your choice sticks per device.
- **Global dice roller** (⚄ in the top bar): opens a panel with 7
  clickable dice (d4 → d100) for one-click rolls, plus an Advanced
  section for *count × die + modifier* with an optional Target% for
  d100 skill checks. Dice physically tumble across the screen and
  settle on a result. The ⚙ inside the panel lets you customize size,
  dice style (4 themes, different fonts), color, and shadows — all
  saved to your device.

## Stuff that'll save you pain

- **Sign in fast, once**: we use Google sign-in for a reason — no
  passwords to forget. If you sign in from a new device later, your
  same agents pop up.
- **Offline works**: if your connection drops mid-session, a red banner
  appears at the top and the app stops letting you edit (so nothing gets
  silently lost). When you reconnect, the banner goes away and you can
  keep going.
- **KIA is reversible**: you can mark an agent Killed In Action and
  everything locks up with a nice red stamp. If the GM changes their
  mind, hit **REVIVE AGENT** on the top banner.
- **Print it**: sidebar has a ⎙ icon per agent — opens a cleanly-
  formatted DD-315 dossier you can print, save as PDF, or hand to the
  Handler.

## If it breaks

- Doesn't load? Hard-refresh (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+R) or close and reopen the
  app. Stale cache is the usual suspect.
- Can't sign in? Tell me the exact error message (screenshot is best)
  — I can usually fix the Google-side config in a minute.
- Your agent disappeared? It's almost always a sign-in-as-the-wrong-
  Google-account thing. Sign out (top right), sign back in with the
  account you used originally.

Anything weirder than that, just text me. Have fun out there.
